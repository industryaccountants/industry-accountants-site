Service Agreement

The Industry Accountants agrees to provide professional accounting, tax, payroll, bookkeeping, and related financial services to the Client as selected during onboarding. Services will be performed by qualified professionals with due care and diligence in accordance with applicable professional standards.

Privacy Policy

All client information, documents, and financial data are treated with strict confidentiality. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. The Firm will not sell, share, or disclose client information to third parties except as required by law or with explicit client consent.

Electronic Communications Consent

By signing this agreement, the Client consents to receive electronic communications including invoices, receipts, status updates, and important notices via email and through the secure client portal.

Fee Agreement

Fees for services are determined after review of the client's specific requirements. A proposal will be provided prior to invoicing. Work begins only after payment is received. All fees are due upon receipt unless otherwise agreed in writing.
