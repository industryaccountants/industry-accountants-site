Audit Assessment

Facing an insurance premium audit? We review your payroll records, reconcile your classifications, and represent your business through every step of the audit process.

Records Review

We audit your payroll records, employee classifications, and reported exposures to ensure everything aligns with your original policy estimates.

Classification & Reconciliation

We reconcile payroll figures, verify employee vs. contractor classifications, and identify overcharges that could reduce your final premium adjustment.

Audit Representation

We represent your business directly with the insurance carrier, challenge inaccurate findings, and negotiate the final premium adjustment on your behalf.
