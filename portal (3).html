Service Agreement

The Industry Accountants ("The Firm") agrees to provide professional accounting, tax, payroll, bookkeeping, and related financial services to the Client as selected during onboarding. Services will be performed by qualified professionals with due care and diligence in accordance with applicable professional standards.

Privacy Policy

All client information, documents, and financial data are treated with strict confidentiality. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. The Firm will not sell, share, or disclose client information to third parties except as required by law or with explicit client consent.

Electronic Communications Consent

By signing this agreement, the Client consents to receive electronic communications including invoices, receipts, status updates, and important notices via email and through the secure client portal.

Fee Agreement

Fees for services are as quoted and agreed upon during the service selection process. A deposit may be required before work commences. Final invoices will be issued upon completion of services. All fees are due upon receipt unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Limitation of Liability

The Firm's liability shall be limited to fees paid for the specific service giving rise to any claim. The Firm is not liable for decisions made by the Client based on professional advice provided.
