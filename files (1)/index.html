Your Data Is Safe With Us

Security you can trust with

AES-256 Encryption

Your Social Security Number and sensitive financial data are encrypted with the same standard used by banks and the U.S. government before they ever touch our servers.

Private by Design

Every client account is completely isolated. Your data is only visible to you and your assigned accountant. No one else — not even our own staff — can access your files without your consent.

IRS Safeguards Compliant

Our data handling practices align with IRS Publication 4557 and the FTC Safeguards Rule, which govern how tax preparers must protect client information.

Your portal automatically logs you out after 20 minutes of inactivity. Even if you forget to sign out, your account stays protected.

Secure Document Vault

Every document you upload lives in a private, encrypted storage vault accessible only to you. Files are transmitted over 256-bit SSL and stored in an isolated environment.
